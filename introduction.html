Introduction¶

About the Package¶

kemitter is a Python package designed to load, process, and visualize experimental measurements collected by wide-angle energy-momentum spectroscopy, a laboratory technique that can simultaneously resolve the energy, momentum, and polarization distributions of light emission for simple photonic structures.
